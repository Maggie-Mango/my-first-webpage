Astro & Jack

Adopted November 2019 from San Francisco SPCA

Astro

Astro is the most hyper of the pair. He is always seen with a mouse in his mouth and loves to play fetch with anyone who visits. That's water by the way.



Jack

Jack is the "big brother" of the pair. He takes life slower and loves to play with with feather teasers. He cannot jump very high unlike his brother.
